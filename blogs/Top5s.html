Top 5s of 2016

With one of my goals for the New Year is to write more, I figured a perfect way to start 2017 was to talk about all the things I loved in 2016. This is also a way to remind myself that while this year was full of bummers when it comes to the world around me, 2016 was a year of considerable personal growth and learning experiences. There are always a positives to pull from any experience , its our job to take what we learned and turn them in to something better. So lets start off these lists with the top five things I learned based on experiences I have had in 2016.

  1. 1. *The Things you can accomplish while being genuine, will be the most satisfying.*
Its sounds like a no brainer, but the feeling of knowing that you were 100% true to yourself when you succeed in something is probably one of the best feelings in the world
  1. 2. *Don’t waiver, make a decision and move forward 100% with it*
This was something I didn’t do, that now looking back would have made a certain transition in my life SOOO much easier. Its so important to know what you want before making a decision and if that time isn’t taken to try and explore the options at hand, indecision and hesitance come into play and you could end up making an impulse decision and not be properly prepared for what comes next.
  1. 3. *Stay open minded*
This past year I have tried to welcome more new experiences by saying yes to more things. This has resulted in things new networking opportunities, job interviews and freelance clients, but it also has lead me to experience things like Philadelphia Drag Scene, Surfing, and living on my own.
  1. 4. *Relationships will surprise you*
I have always been a social guy and have always tried to have many groups of friends with different and eclectic interests. Because of this however, it does mean I unintentionally go without seeing some friends for long periods of time. While it makes it hard to maintain friendships its a always a great feeling to reconnect. I have also harbored new relationships this year that are even more friendships with people that I not only relate with but also we are able to celebrate our differences and drive healthy debates.
  1. 5. *Things happen, time passes*
This is another one of those things that I think I knew already, but in the heat of panic and frustration it becomes easy to forget. Time keeps moving none of my personal issues are ever has bad as they seem. Take those negatives from the bad and using them to create some good. Now for the fun lists :D
